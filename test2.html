Guide: Good afternoon everybody and welcome to Riverside Industrial Village...

Riverside Village was a good place to start an industry because it had water, raw materials and also an abundance of local fuels, such as coal and firewood [11].

The metal industry was established at Riverside Village by local craftsmen [12] who lived in the area... By the seventeenth and eighteenth centuries the region's rivers supported more than 160 [13] water-powered mills.

Now I’m going to give you a plan of the site. That’s Woodside [14] Road, which is the entrance to the village.

As you come through the entrance, immediately on your right is the Ticket Office [15]. In front of you is the car park, and on the left, next to the gate, is the Gift Shop [16].

The large building at the top, facing the entrance, is the main Workshop [17]. In the top right-hand corner is the Showroom [18].

On one side of the Grinding Shop is the Cafe [19], where you can get drinks and snacks.

Finally, the row of buildings on the left, near the river, are the cottages [20]. These were built for the workers.
