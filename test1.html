Tapescript (Full Transcript)

Official: Good morning.

Student: Good morning. How can I help you?

Official: I understand that the school organises trips to different places...

Student: Yes, we run five every month... places of historical interest, and also which offer a variety of shopping [1] because our students always ask about that. And then we go for ones where we know there are guided tours [2] because this gives a good focus.

Official: How much do they cost?

Student: It varies... between five and fifteen pounds a head. Oh, and we do offer to arrange special trips if there are more than 12 [3] people.

Student: You sign your name on the notice board [4]. Do you know where it is?

Student: Right, well... The first one is St Ives, that's on the 13th of February [5]...

Student: Then there’s a London trip... the optional extra is the Tower of London [6].

Student: After that there’s Bristol [7] on the 3rd of March. The optional extra is a visit to the S.S. Great Britain.

Student: And the last one is to Bath on the 23rd of March... visit the American Museum [8]. Well worth a visit.

Student: For more information, have a look in the student newspaper [9]. Or have a word with my assistant. Her name is Jane Yentob [10].
